Currently under progress

A project launched by the Jumbo code team

The project uses MongoDB and nodeJS for the backend, Angular for the front end desktop and ionic for the mobile front end.

The app facilitates the check-in and check-out process for volunteers and notifies them about their roles and where they should be during their shifts. Hence, it has a back end that processes the data, front end desktop for the administrator to edit the information, and the front end mobile that displays the most up-to-date information to the volunteers.
